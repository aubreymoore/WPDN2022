Cycas micronesica trees being killed by ACS
Decline of Guam's endemic Cycas micronesica

Impact of Asian cycad scale

  • Cycas micronesica went from being being the most abundant tree in Guam's forests in 2002 to being placed on the US Threatened and Endangered Species List in 2015.
  • The population is still in decline and no reproduction is taking place.
coconut rhinoceros beetle, *Oryctes rhinoceros* (CRB) ![](images/crbg.png)
coconut palms being killed by CRB

Impact of coconut rhinoceros beetle

  • We don't have an estimate of how many coconut palms have been killed.
  • About 20% coconut palms are currently showing signs of CRB damage.
